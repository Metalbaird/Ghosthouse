You walk through the darkness and notice that a Fox head and Stag head are mounted on the deteriorating wall. There is a plaque under each trophy.
Written under the Stag is "The Valiant Stag".
The plaque under the Fox reads "The Cunning Fox".
Both of the heads are in strangely good condition and both have their mouths open wider than naturally possible.
The mouths are wide enough for a hand to reach in...


Reach into the Stag to show your bravery?

Reach into the Foxes mouth to show your cunning?

Head back to the study?
