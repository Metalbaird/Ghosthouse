A Haunted Escape

The night is dark, the road is long, and it has been a longer day
You are walking home and feeling on edge.
You pass the old abandoned mansion at the end of the road
Countless horror stories come from that place.
Lights flicker...
Thunderous thuds are heard..
the house shakes with disembodied screams...

You try to put those stories out of your head as you walk past,
but suddenly you feel yourself slipping into a deep sleep.
Each step you take weighs heavier on your mind, and your vision goes to black...
